The Sorsu E-Voting System uses short, clear notifications to guide the user through every critical stage of the election.

Notifications begin in the Pre-Voting phase, alerting users that Voter registration is now open! and sending an urgent reminder like Final day to register for the Sorsu elections! as the deadline approaches.

Once the polls open, Active Voting notifications immediately inform the user that Sorsu E-Voting is LIVE! and create urgency with reminders such as Only 12 hours left to vote! Finally, in the Post-Voting phase, the system confirms major actions with alerts like Your vote has been successfully recorded and keeps users informed on the results status, sending a final notification when Official Results are OUT!

This phased approach ensures users are constantly aware of their next action and the election's current status.
